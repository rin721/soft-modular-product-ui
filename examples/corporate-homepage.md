# Example: Corporate Homepage

## Input Requirement

Create a corporate homepage for a B2B media workflow platform. Audience: operations teams and creator partnerships. Primary goal: book a demo. Tone: corporate but still friendly.

## Skill Rules Used

- Adaptation rules: corporate tone, reduced decoration, possible blue or softened pink accent.
- Layout grammar: banner hero, feature sections, stat cards, CTA, compact footer.
- Components: Hero, Button, Section, Card, Feature List, Form, Footer.
- Interaction rules: standard hover/focus/active, reduced playful loops.

## Generation Strategy

Keep the distilled structure but lower the emotional volume. Use fewer decorative shapes, more direct copy, and clearer enterprise sections. Preserve the slim navigation, small-radius cards, tokenized states, and soft elevation.

## Structure Draft

1. App-like top nav or left rail depending on product depth.
2. Hero: product name, enterprise value prop, demo CTA, secondary product tour.
3. Trust/stat row: three stat cards with accent numbers.
4. Workflow section: ingest, review, publish, measure.
5. Product preview: original dashboard mockups in 16:9 cards.
6. Security/operations info band.
7. Demo CTA section with compact form.
8. Footer with docs, support, legal, status.

## Token Notes

- Accent may remain `#f06e8e` or adapt to a less playful brand color.
- Keep `4px` badges and `6px` cards.
- Use softer decoration: one pale oversized icon or diagonal block.
- Motion: keep default `0.25s`; avoid looping decorative rotation.

## Quality Checks

- Tone reads professional, not childish.
- Structure remains token-driven and responsive.
- Form has labels, focus stripe, invalid state, and disabled state.
- No source-site assets or category data are copied.
- Inferred footer and CTA behavior is labeled if delivered as a spec.
